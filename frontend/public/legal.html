When you search for a flight, the flight number is transmitted to our backend server solely to retrieve publicly available flight status data from third-party providers. We do not log, retain, or associate flight searches with any personal identifier. Query logs, if any, are retained for no longer than 24 hours for system error diagnostics only.

6. Third-Party Services

Flight Pickup integrates the following third-party services, each governed by their own independent privacy policies. We are not responsible for the data practices of these providers:

  • AviationStack — flight status data (flight number is transmitted to their API)
  • OpenWeatherMap — weather data for destination airports (airport city name transmitted)
  • API Ninjas — airport information lookup (IATA code transmitted)
  • Leaflet.js / OpenStreetMap — map tile rendering for the flight tracker
  • Google Fonts — typeface assets served from Google's CDN
  • Unsplash — background photography served via their CDN

Flight Pickup does not use cookies for tracking, profiling, or advertising. No third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking cookies are placed on your device. We do not use pixel tracking, fingerprinting, or any other covert identification technology.
